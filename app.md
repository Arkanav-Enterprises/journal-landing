## Full App Store description

Your phone opens a hundred times a day. Most of those opens end in the same scroll.

Journal Lock puts a lock on your most distracting apps and hands you the key only after you write. A few lines in the morning, a page before bed, whatever the day needs. Finish your entry and your apps open. Skip it and they stay shut.

It turns the urge to grab your phone into a moment to check in with yourself instead. Over a week you scroll less. Over a month you have a real writing habit and a streak you will not want to break.

Built for people who want their attention back:

- Block the apps that pull you in, on your terms
- Write to earn your screen time instead of losing it
- Keep a daily streak that makes the habit stick
- Watch your focus grow as the doomscrolling fades

Watch your streak grow day by day. Your mascot companion grows with you — journal daily to keep it thriving, miss a day and it starts to shrink.

- Daily streak tracking with milestone celebrations
- Personalized journal prompts that evolve with you
- Mood and alignment check-ins to track how you feel over time
- Home screen widget showing your latest entry

**The science:** Neuroscientists call it "affect labeling" — writing about your emotions calms the brain's stress center. Consistent journaling reduces anxiety, improves focus, builds emotional resilience.

**Privacy:** All entries stored locally on device. No cloud uploads, no data mining, no ads.

**Pricing:** Free. Journal Lock Pro — $5.99/week or $79.99/year (3-day free trial) — unlimited entries, premium themes, advanced features.

3. **Daily prompts** — "today's prompt" personalized, evolves over time
4. **Mood check-ins** — Awful/Bad/Okay/Good/Great scale on every entry, average mood chart
5. **Streaks** — day streak with weekly calendar, milestone celebrations
6. **Mascot companion** — sprout character with HP/levels: +2 HP per journal day, −1 per missed day
7. **Daily summary** — "Here's what you've built today": streak, mood, apps blocked count
8. **Journal passcode** — separate passcode protects the journal itself
9. **Home screen widget** — latest entry on home screen
10. **Local-only storage** — no cloud, no accounts, no ads
